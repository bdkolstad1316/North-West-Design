Who we are

Local shop. Twenty-five years. One zip code.

We've been locally-owned in Clarkston since 2001, proudly working out of our building just off Fleshman Way. From screen printing, to embroidery, to graphic design, and more — we create custom gear for everyone, from large companies to one-off gifts.

We're local, easy to reach, and quick to respond. We know our products, deliver quick turnaround, and take pride in quality work that looks good and lasts. Whether you need a few shirts or a full company order, we make the process simple from start to finish.
