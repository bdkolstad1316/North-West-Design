Who We Are

Family shop. Twenty-five years. One zip code.

We've been a family-owned shop in Clarkston since 2001, working out of a building just off Fleshman Way. We print t-shirts, embroider ball caps, cut vinyl, and design logos for everyone from little league teams to fun runs to local businesses that need a thousand hoodies by Friday.

We do the work in-house. We answer our own phones. And we'll tell you straight if your idea isn't going to print well. That's it. That's the pitch.
