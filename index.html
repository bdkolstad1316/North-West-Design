What we do

Four things, all done right.

We're not trying to be everything. We do custom apparel and graphics for the Lewis-Clark Valley and beyond — and we've been doing it since the year the iPod came out.

01 / SP

Screenprinting

We pull ink, not punches.

One shirt or ten thousand. Sharp inks, durable cure, color matched to whatever your designer dreamed up. The thing we're known for.

02 / EM

Embroidery

We stitch fast, not loose.

Hats, polos, jackets, blankets. Stitched in-house — not farmed out. It looks professional because it is.

03 / CV

Vinyl Decals

We cut vinyl, not corners.

Truck windows, helmets, tumblers, storefronts. If the surface is smooth, we'll put your logo on it.

Who we are

Family shop. Twenty-five years. One zip code.

We've been a family-owned shop in Clarkston since 2001, working out of a building just off Fleshman Way. We print t-shirts, embroider ball caps, cut vinyl, and design logos for everyone from little league teams to fun runs to local businesses that need a thousand hoodies by Friday.

We do the work in-house. We answer our own phones. And we'll tell you straight if your idea isn't going to print well. That's it. That's the pitch.
